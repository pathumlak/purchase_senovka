Executive Summary

What the system is, who it serves, and why it was built

The Senovka Plastics ERP is a fully custom-built, web-based Enterprise Resource Planning system designed specifically for the operational needs of Senovka Plastics & Hardware. It replaces manual record-keeping and disconnected spreadsheets with a single integrated platform that connects every area of the business — customers, orders, production, billing, purchasing, and finance.

The system is accessible from any device with a web browser, requires no software installation, and is secured with role-based user access so that each team member sees and can do only what their role permits.

What Problems This System Solves

Business Problem How the ERP Solves It
Manual billing on paper leads to errors and lost records Auto-numbered digital bills with full payment tracking and print-ready invoices
No visibility into what customers owe or have in credit Live customer balance system — automatically updates on every transaction
Difficulty tracking cheque payments and bounces Full cheque lifecycle management: received → cleared / bounced, with balance reversal
No way to know if stock is enough to fulfil pending orders Live production needs calculator — shows exactly how much to produce per product
Material purchase weights cannot be verified against invoices Scale weight verification with automatic discrepancy calculation per purchase
Petty cash tracking is done in a notebook with no running balance Digital monthly ledger with opening balance, running totals, and PDF/Excel export
Supplier invoices and received goods are not reconciled Supply receipt module that updates product stock and optionally applies credit to the supplier's customer account
No limit on how much credit a customer can run up Per-customer maximum outstanding limit enforced at bill creation, set by SuperAdmin only

Technology Platform

Built on Django (Python) with a responsive Tailwind CSS frontend. The system runs on a Linux server and is accessed via any modern web browser. Reports can be exported to Excel (.xlsx) or PDF at the click of a button in every module.

Customer Management

Centralised customer profiles with live balance and custom pricing

Every customer in the system has a complete profile that tracks their contact details, account balance, and custom pricing. The system maintains a single intelligent balance field that automatically distinguishes between money the customer owes and credit they have built up.

Balance System

🟢 Positive Balance (Credit)

When a customer's balance is positive (e.g. Rs. +500), it means the customer has credit with the business — they have overpaid or made an advance payment. This credit is automatically deducted from their next bill.

🔴 Negative Balance (Outstanding)

When a customer's balance is negative (e.g. Rs. −500), it means the customer owes us money — they have unpaid bills. This outstanding amount is automatically added to their next bill's net payable.

Custom Product Pricing

Each customer can have a unique price set for any product. When creating a bill or booking for that customer, the system automatically loads their custom prices. If no custom price is set for a product, the default price applies. Pricing lists can be exported to Excel or PDF for customer reference.

Outstanding Credit Limit (SuperAdmin)

Credit Limit Protection

The SuperAdmin can set a maximum outstanding limit per customer. If a new PAY_LATER bill would cause the customer's outstanding balance to exceed this limit, the system blocks the bill from being created and shows a warning message. This prevents customers from running up unlimited debt and protects the business from bad debts.

Bill Settlement (Pending Bills)

For any bill with an outstanding balance, the Settle Payment screen allows recording additional payments — by cash, cheque, or applying customer credit — until the bill is fully paid. Each payment is recorded individually with a date, keeping a full payment history on the bill.

Printed Invoice

Every bill has a print-ready A4 invoice that shows: company header with logo, customer details, itemised product list, subtotal / discount / bill total, customer balance adjustment, net payable, payment history, and signature lines. The invoice automatically adjusts its font size to fit up to 10 line items on a single page.

Live Production Needs Calculator

What the Production Needs Panel Shows

As products and quantities are added to a booking, the system immediately queries the database and shows for each product:

  • In Stock — current available quantity in the warehouse
  • Other Pending Orders — total quantity of this product already booked in other pending (unconfirmed) orders
  • This Order — quantity being entered in the current booking
  • Total Required — sum of all pending orders + this order
  • Production Needed — how much more must be produced to fulfil all orders (highlighted in red if > 0, green if stock is sufficient)

This gives the production team an instant view of exactly how many units to manufacture before confirming orders, eliminating shortfalls and production surprises.

Quotation Export

Any pending booking can be exported as a professional quotation document in both PDF and Excel formats, ready to send to the customer for approval before confirmation.

Material Purchasing

Raw material purchase recording with scale weight verification

The material purchasing module records every raw material purchase from suppliers. A key feature is scale weight verification — the ability to record the actual weight measured on the business's own scale and compare it against the supplier's stated quantity, automatically calculating any discrepancy.

Scale Weight Verification

Protecting Against Weight Discrepancies

When the business receives a material delivery, the actual weight is measured on the company's own scale. The system records this scale weight alongside the supplier's stated quantity and automatically calculates:

  • Weight Discrepancy = Scale Weight − Invoice Quantity
  • Discrepancy Percentage = (Discrepancy ÷ Invoice Quantity) × 100

This provides auditable evidence when a supplier is consistently delivering less than invoiced — protecting the business from overpaying for materials it did not receive.

Supplier–Customer Dual Role

When a Supplier is Also a Customer

A supplier can be linked to an existing Customer profile. When a supply receipt is recorded for such a supplier, the system can automatically apply the supply cost as a credit to that customer's account balance. This handles the case where a party both buys from and supplies to Senovka Plastics — the debt is offset automatically.

Supply Receipt Reversal

If a supply receipt needs to be deleted (for example if it was entered in error), the system automatically reverses all effects: stock quantities are reduced back to their previous levels, and any customer credit that was applied is reversed.

Petty Cash Ledger

Daily cash management with running balance and monthly statements

The petty cash module replaces the physical cash book with a digital ledger. Every cash movement in or out of the petty cash fund is recorded, and the system maintains a continuously running balance from the very first entry to today.

Cash Entry Types

💰 Cash In

Money coming into the petty cash fund — top-ups, reimbursements received, or cash refunds. Each entry records the date, reference number, amount, and purpose.

💸 Cash Out

Money paid out for small business expenses — stationery, transport, minor repairs. Each entry records the date, reference number, amount, and what it was spent on.

Monthly Ledger View

ColumnDescription
DateDate of the transaction
ReferenceTransaction reference number
Purpose / DescriptionWhat the money was for
Cash InAmount received (positive)
Cash OutAmount paid out (negative)
Running BalanceCumulative balance after each entry — continuous from the very first record

The monthly view shows the opening balance carried forward from the previous month, all transactions for the selected month, and the closing balance at month end. Month navigation arrows allow moving backward and forward through the records.

Optional Receipt Image

Each petty cash entry optionally supports uploading a photo of the receipt or bill, creating an auditable paper trail directly within the system.

Monthly Statement Export

Each monthly ledger can be exported to Excel or PDF with full formatting — including the opening balance, all entries with running balances, monthly totals, and the closing balance. Suitable for accountant review or internal audit.

User Roles & Access Control

Two-tier permission system to protect sensitive data and operations

The system uses role-based access control (RBAC). Every user must log in and is assigned one of two roles. This ensures that sensitive financial operations and business configuration are restricted to authorised users only.

👑 SuperAdmin

Full system control — typically the business owner or senior manager

  • Create, edit, and delete anything in the system
  • Edit bills after creation
  • Cancel bills and reverse all effects
  • Set customer maximum outstanding credit limits
  • Create and assign tasks to any user
  • Manage all user accounts and roles
  • Access all reports and exports
  • Configure company settings and branding
  • View full activity log of all user actions
👤 Regular User

Day-to-day operational staff — sales, production, admin

  • Create bills and bookings
  • View and search customers
  • Record petty cash entries
  • Log daily machine runs
  • Record material purchases
  • Record supply receipts
  • View and complete assigned tasks
  • Export reports for their area
  • Cannot edit bills or cancel transactions

Audit & Activity Logging

Every significant action in the system is logged automatically — who did it, what they did, when, and on which record. This creates a complete audit trail and allows the SuperAdmin to review all activity across every module.

Key Business Benefits

How the ERP system directly improves business operations

📊 Complete Financial Visibility

At any moment, the business can see exactly who owes how much, what is in credit, what bills are pending, and what cheques are outstanding — all in one system, in real time.

🔒 Credit Risk Protection

The per-customer outstanding limit blocks sales on credit beyond approved thresholds, preventing bad debts before they happen rather than chasing them afterwards.

🏭 Production Planning Intelligence

The live production needs calculator prevents stockouts by showing exactly how much to manufacture before confirming orders — connecting the sales team directly to production planning.

✉️ Professional Customer Communications

Instantly generate polished A4 invoices and Excel/PDF quotations for customers, reflecting the company's professional image and eliminating handwritten paperwork.

🔍 Full Traceability & Audit Trail

Every transaction, edit, cancellation, and login is logged with the user, time, and details. Management can always answer "who did what and when" without relying on memory.

⚖️ Material Purchase Accuracy

The scale weight verification feature means every delivery is physically checked and any weight discrepancy is recorded — protecting the business from being overcharged for materials not received.

The system runs in a web browser — no installation required. Staff can access it from desktop computers, laptops, tablets, or smartphones from the office or the factory floor.

🧾 Cheque Bounce Recovery

When a cheque bounces, a single click reverses the payment, restores the customer's balance, reopens the bill, and preserves the complete audit trail — no manual corrections needed.
